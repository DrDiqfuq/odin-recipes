Brisket

This is brisket. It's really good. I smoke using hickory chunks/pellets, because that's my preferred beef flavor profile. Honestly pairs best with a hickory barbecue sauce, but just about any will work. The rub is pretty damn versatile.

What You'll Need

  • Smoker
  • Pellets/chunks of preferred wood
  • Aluminum pan
  • Water
  • Spray bottle
  • Filet knife
  • Cutting board
  • Aluminum foil or butcher paper
  • Shrink wrap
  • Meat thermometer probe
  • Preferred impliment for removing brisket from smoker

Ingredients

  • 1 12-18lbs brisket, whole
  • 2 cups Kosher salt
  • 2 cups Coarse black pepper
  • 3 tbsp Cayenne pepper powder
  • 1 cup onion powder
  • 1 cup garlic powder
  • 2tbsp ground coriander
  • Yellow mustard
  • Water
  • Apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Trim the brisket (as one does).
  2. In a bowl, mix your spices. Stir with a fork to evenly mix throughout.
  3. Rub Brisket down with a thin layer of yellow mustard. The mustard flavor burns off, but the mustard acts as a binding agent for your rub.
  4. Shake your rub evenly and generously on the brisket, patting it in with your hand, rather than rubbing it in.
  5. Tightly wrap your brisket in shrink wrap, then refrigerate for 24 hours.
  6. Mix 1/2 cup water and 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar in your spray bottle.
  7. Heat your smoker to 180 degrees Farenheit.
  8. Insert your thermometer probe into the brisket.
  9. Fill your aluminum pan 3/4 of the way with water. Place on the coals/grease trap rack, depending on smoker model.
  10. Place brisket into smoker and close.
  11. Mist brisket with vinegar/water mix every 45-60 minutes.
  12. When internal temp reaches 170 degrees, remove brisket from smoker and tightly wrap with aluminum foil or butcher paper.
  13. Increase smoker temp to 225 degrees and continue smoking until internal temp reaches 203-206.
  14. Remove from smoker and cut in 1/4 thick strips, against the grain.
  15. Alternatively, cut into cubes and serve on Hawaiian Rolls (like a slider) with barbecue sauce.
  16. Promptly die and go to heaven because the cow you just smoked was a holy symbol, elevating the flesh and blood circulating throughout you to near-deific status.
  17. Conduct a pseudo-rapture (not like the biblical one, though, because that's pretty different).
